## Posting tips

1. **Best time to post:** 7–9 PM Nepal time (after dinner, peak FB use).
2. **Add a screenshot** — open the tool, fill a sample sheet, screenshot
   the live preview pane. Visual posts get 2–3x more engagement.
3. **Pin a comment** with the link separately — sometimes FB
   deprioritizes posts with URLs in the body. The comment workaround:
   "Here's the link 👉 https://forgesaroj.github.io/oddy-printer/"
4. **Tag local business groups:** "Nepal Small Business", "Kathmandu
   Shopkeepers", "Nepali Entrepreneurs Network", etc.
5. **Reshare to your story** with a 24-hour countdown — boosts reach.
6. **First 30 minutes are critical** — ask 2–3 friends to like/comment
   immediately so the algorithm picks it up.
